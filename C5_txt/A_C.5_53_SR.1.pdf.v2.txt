Election of the Chairman
1. Mr. Chowdhury (Bangladesh) nominated Mr. Abelian (Armenia) for the office of Chairman.
2. In the absence of further nominations and in accordance with rule 103 of the rules of procedure, the Temporary Chairman said that he would take it that the Committee wished to elect Mr. Abelian (Armenia) Chairman.
3. Mr. Abelian (Armenia) was elected Chairman by acclamation.
The meeting rose at 4.05p.m.
