Election of the Chairman
1. Mr. de Rojas (Venezuela) nominated Mr. Asadi (Islamic Republic of Iran) for the office of Chairman.
2. In the absence of further nominations and in accordance with rule 103 of the rules of procedure, the Temporary Chairman said that he would take it that the Committee wished to elect Mr. Asadi (Islamic Republic of Iran) Chairman.
3. Mr. Asadi (Islamic Republic of Iran) was elected Chairman by acclamation.
The meeting rose at 3.55p.m.
