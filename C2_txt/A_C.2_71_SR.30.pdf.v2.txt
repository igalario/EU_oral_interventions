The meeting was called to order at noon.
Agenda item 5: Election of the officers of the Main Committees
1. The Chair said that, in accordance with rule 99 (a) of the rules of procedure, the meeting had been convened for the purpose of electing the Chair and other members of the Bureau of the Committee for the seventy-second session of the General Assembly.
2. The nomination of Mr. Jurgenson (Estonia) for the office of Chair had been endorsed by the Group of Eastern European States. In the absence of further nominations, and in accordance with rule 103 of the rules of procedure, he would take it that the Committee wished to elect Mr. Jurgenson (Estonia) Chair.
3. Mr. Jurgenson (Estonia) was elected Chair by acclamation.
4. Mr. Jurgenson (Estonia) said that he was committed to working with delegations to address global challenges, which were increasing in number and severity.
5. The Chair said that the nominations of
Mr. Menelaou (Cyprus), Ms. Louis (Saint Lucia) and Ms. Bruell-Melchior (Monaco) for the three offices of Vice-Chair had been endorsed by the Group of Asia Pacific States, the Group of Latin American and Caribbean States and the Group of Western European and Other States, respectively.
6. Mr. Menelaou (Cyprus), Ms. Louis (Saint Lucia) and Ms. Bruell-Melchior (Monaco) were elected Vice-Chairs by acclamation.
7. The Chair said that the nomination of
Ms. Chanda (Zambia) for the office of Rapporteur had been endorsed by the Group of African States.
8. Ms. Chanda (Zambia) was elected Rapporteur by acclamation.
The meeting rose at 12.10 p.m.
