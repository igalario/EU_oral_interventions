In the absence of Mr. Bowler (Malawi), Mr. Medan (Croatia), Vice-Chair, took the Chair.
The meeting was called to order at 3.10 p.m.
Agenda item 56: Comprehensive review of the whole question of peacekeeping operations in all their aspects (continued) (A/70/19; A/C.4/70/L.24)
1. The Chair recalled that the Committee had held a general debate on agenda item 56 in October and November 2015. The Special Committee on Peacekeeping Operations had adopted its report (A/70/19) at its 2016 session. The report was now before the Committee at the resumed session.
2. Mr. Kandeel (Egypt), speaking as Rapporteur of the Special Committee on Peacekeeping Operations and introducing its report (A/70/19), said that the Special Committee had met from 16 February to 11 March 2016. After the general debate, the Working Group of the Whole had met to discuss and finalize the proposals, recommendations and conclusions of the Special Committee.
Draft resolution A/C.4/70/L.24: Comprehensive review of the whole question ofpeacekeeping operations in all their aspects
3. The Chair said that draft resolution A/C.4/70/L.24 had no programme budget implications.
4. Mr. Kandeel (Egypt) introduced the draft resolution, which was based on similar resolutions adopted in recent years. Under paragraphs 2 and 3, the General Assembly would endorse the proposals, recommendations and conclusions of the Special Committee contained in paragraphs 19 to 369 of its report, and would urge Member States, the Secretariat and relevant organs of the United Nations to take all necessary steps to implement them.
5. Draft resolution A/C.4/70/L.24 was adopted.
The meeting rose at 3.20 p.m.
